Stage 4 — CCB Meeting & Decision (Approvals During Meeting)

Owners: CCB Voting Members + Approval Groups · SLA: Day 10

All required approvals are collected during the CCB meeting. Approval groups review and sign off during the session. CCB voting members then deliberate and vote. Outcomes: Approved, Approved with Conditions, Deferred, or Rejected.

🆕 What Changed in v2.1

  • Pre-CCB Approvals removed — no separate approval collection stage
  • Approvals during CCB meeting — all approval groups sign off during the meeting
  • Completeness Review → CCB scheduling — passing review triggers CCB scheduling directly
  • 7 phases (down from 8), 34 email notifications, 7 gates (G1–G7)
