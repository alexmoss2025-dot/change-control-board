📘 Quick Reference Guide

Change Categories

Standard: Pre-approved, low-risk changes
Normal: Regular changes requiring CCB review
Major: Significant changes with high impact
Emergency: Urgent changes to restore service

Status Workflow

Submitted → Under Review → Approved/Rejected → Implemented → PIR Complete
All changes must complete PIR within 5 business days of implementation.

Risk Levels

Low: Minimal impact, easy rollback
Medium: Moderate impact, some complexity
High: Significant impact, difficult rollback
Critical: Major business impact
