❓ How to Use This Flowchart — v2.1

🆕 What Changed in v2.1

  • Pre-CCB Approvals removed — no separate approval collection phase
  • Approvals during CCB Meeting — all approval groups sign off during the CCB meeting
  • Completeness Review → CCB Scheduling — passing review triggers direct CCB scheduling
  • 7 phases (was 8), 7 gates (was 8), 34 notifications (was 43)
  • Faster cycle time — ~5 days faster for Normal changes

Reading the Flowchart

This swimlane diagram shows the complete CR workflow across 7 phases (columns) and 7 roles (rows). Flow goes left → right.

Swimlane Roles (7 Rows)

  • 👤 Change Owner — Initiates CR (Sec 1–2) and closes (Sec 8)
  • 🔧 Technical Lead — Technical details + QA (Sec 3–7) UPDATED
  • 🔍 Product Owner + CCB Lead — Completeness Review NEW
  • ✅ Approval Groups — Security, Infrastructure, Business, Compliance (approve during CCB meeting)
  • 🎯 CCB Facilitator — Validates, facilitates, approves PIR
  • 🗳️ CCB Voting Members — Decision authority at gates
  • ⚙️ System/Automated — Emails, signatures, archiving
